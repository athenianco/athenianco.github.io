Workflow
========

We are entirely remote, with flexible hours, and the tools are the key to our productivity.

We are using Slack for instant messaging and asynchronous daily standups, Zoom for meetings, Notion for the knowledge database, Google Docs Editors for office applications, Atlassian JIRA for task management, incident.io for on-call incident management.

Athenian's development methodology is a fusion of Kanban with Agile. We follow variadic-sized sprints. At the same time, we are allergic to shitcoding and follow best practices.

Culture
=======

We've established a strong, engineer-first development culture at Athenian. We believe that people are >> formalities and processes. We look for "kind-hearted warriors".

We respect work-life balance. Our team doesn't go through work crunches, exploding deadlines, or busy weekends. Also, we try to minimize the frequency of meetings. Currently, we've got 0.5 company-wide, 0.5 one-on-one, and 1.0 engineering weekly meetings.

We believe in transparency and openness. The CEO doesn't hide dirty secrets, our financials are fully available internally, and every team reports progress regularly.

Athenian is a place of equal opportunities for everyone, no matter your religion, race, gender, or sexual orientation. We strive to build a diverse and inclusive team.

Career growth
=============

We strongly believe that engineers don't have to become managers to self-develop and have their salary raised. At Athenian, we offer two career tracks compensated equivalently: Individual Contributor and Manager. Both tracks have multiple steps that optimally adapt to your progression. The sky is the limit!

If at any point you find yourself moving to our Manager track, we have you covered: don't forget what our business is. All the engineering management best practices we gathered over the recent years are available, as well as mentorship from more senior managers.

We encourage and offer support you in upgrading your hard skills: visiting and speaking at conferences, reading books, contributing to open-source projects.

We're growing quickly and are building something big together. We'd love to hear from you!
